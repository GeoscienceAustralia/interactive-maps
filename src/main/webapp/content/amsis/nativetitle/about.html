Services supplied by the National Native Title Tribunal.

Custodial geospatial data held by the NNTT consists of those datasets necessary to contribute to the statutory functions associated with Registers and other information, in support of the Native Title Act 1993

Whilst these datasets do not form part of the statutory registers, they enable the visualization and ability to search on these matters.
