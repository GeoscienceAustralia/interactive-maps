This map depicts a collection of marine and coastal datasets and services that are made publically available by Geoscience Australia. Datasets within this application include:

- Marine Sediments (MARS) Database of Australia
- Predicted Mud, Sand and Gravel Content of Australia's Northwest Shelf
- Towed Underwater Video Transects
- Marine Survey Backscatter
- Australian Coastal Sediment Compartments
- Seascapes of Australia's Continenetal Shelf

Geoscience Australia is the Australian Government's national mapping agency, we provide authoritative geographic information services and products to enable evidence-based decision making, deliver government policy, assist industry development needs and support community wellbeing.

Information in this application should not be relied upon as the sole source of information for commercial and operational decisions.
