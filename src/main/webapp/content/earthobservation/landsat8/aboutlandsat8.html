Australia as seen from the spectral bands of the Landsat 8 satellite, operated by the United States Geological Survey. This image combines hundereds of images acquired between 2013 and 2015 into a typical winter view of Australia from space. Geoscience Australia acquires Lndsat data though it's data reception station at Alice Springs. Images were processed on the Autralian Geoscience Data Cube at the National Computational Infrastructure, Australian National University.
