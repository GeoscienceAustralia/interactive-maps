This map depicts a collection of external environmental management and biological datasets and webservices, managed, maintained and distributed by the Department of Enviornment and the CSIRO. Web services available include:

- Commonwealth Marine Resserves symbolized on IUCN Zone (DoE)
- Australia, World Heritage Areas (DoE)
- Collaborative Australian Protected Area's Database 2014 (DoE)
- Directory of Important Wetlands in Australia (DoE)
- Ramsar Wetlands of Australia (DoE)
- National Heritage List (DoE)
- Selected marine protected species established under the EPBC Act (ALA, CSIRO)

Geoscience Australia is the Australian Government's national mapping agency, we provide authoritative geographic information services and products to enable evidence-based decision making, deliver government policy, assist industry development needs and support community wellbeing.

Information in this application should not be relied upon as the sole source of information for commercial and operational decisions.
