DEMs derived from LiDAR data from a number of different elevation projects within MGA zone 54 are contained in this web service. This service will continually be updated with additional datasets.

MDBEP

The current elevation projects in this service are: Murray-Darling Elevation Project 2013-2014 - Namoi1 and Namoi2, Geocat record: 79221, Lower Darling, Geocat record: 78360, Namoi2, Geocat record: 79221
