IMPORTANT NOTICE

The fishery zones in these diagrams are created using the limits and zones from the Australian Maritime Boundaries dataset released in 2006 (AMB2006). Australia's maritime limits and zones were updated in 2014, and released as the AMB2014a dataset. The information shown in these diagrams has not be updated to reflect AMB2014a, therefore some of the fisheries zones will be represented incorrectly. Users must seek advice from the Australian Fisheries Management Authority if they have any questions relating to the diagrams or the data depicted in them.

The data contained in this service is not authoritative and has not been updated since 2006. This web service contains the legacy data found in the Australian Marine Spatial Information System (AMSIS) hosted by Geoscience Australia between 2006 and 2015, with a currency date of 2006. The zones depicted are as-made in 2006, any subsequent amendments will not be shown on these diagrams. Users must seek advice from the Australian Fisheries Management Authority if they have any questions relating to the diagrams or the data depicted in them.
